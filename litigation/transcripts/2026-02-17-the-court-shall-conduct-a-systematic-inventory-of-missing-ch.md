## INVENTORY PROTOCOL ESTABLISHED

The court shall proceed with the following systematic inventory:

1. **Current Capabilities Assessment**
   - Document all existing personality biases and blind spots
   - Map current coverage across domains (security, accessibility, compliance, etc.)
   - Identify gaps in expertise coverage

2. **Critical Missing Traits Inventory**
   - Accessibility advocacy
   - Compliance and regulatory perspective
   - User experience focus
   - Performance optimization
   - Internationalization/localization
   - Data privacy

3. **Implementation Evaluation**
   - For each missing trait, evaluate:
     - Can existing personalities cover this adequately?
     - Is this a blind spot requiring dedicated attention?
     - What would be the impact of adding specialist coverage?

4. **Dynamic vs. Static Analysis**
   - Study the implications of dynamic personality assembly
   - Document pros and cons for institutional memory and consistency
   - Develop hybrid approaches if appropriate

The inventory shall be conducted with the understanding that new permanent members require F5-level justification and unanimous approval.
