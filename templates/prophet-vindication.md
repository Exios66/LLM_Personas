# Prophet Vindication Record

Use when the court acknowledges that the Prophet was correct (e.g. a warned risk materialized or a hail-mary perspective was adopted).

---

## Required updates

1. **state/current.md** — Add or append to `prophetVindications` with:
   - Matter (brief)
   - Date
   - What was vindicated (risk that materialized, or ruling that aligned with Prophet's position)

2. **CHANGELOG.md** — Under "Warned" (or equivalent) category:
   - One-line entry: date, matter, and that Prophet was vindicated.

3. **Narrative (optional)** — One sentence for transcript or state: e.g. "The court notes, with appropriate restraint, that the Prophet had warned of [X]."
