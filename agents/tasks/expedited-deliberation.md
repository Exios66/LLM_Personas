# Task: Expedited Deliberation

**Agent:** MORNINGSTAR (or compatible deliberative agent)  
**Source:** `core/procedures.md` (Expedited Deliberation)

## Objective

Produce a brief deliberation transcript for F2 or time-sensitive matters: matter, one-line positions, vote, ruling.

## Steps

1. **Matter** — State the matter in one line.
2. **Positions** — Architect, Engineer, Debugger, Prophet, Counsel (1 line each).
3. **Vote** — Tally (YES-NO-ABSTAIN).
4. **Ruling** — Decision in 1–2 sentences.

## Outputs

- Short transcript in markdown.
- No full cross-examination or Consultant; no Hail-Mary required.
